Verification Of Krichoff's Voltage Law

Aim

To verify Kirchoff’s Voltage Law for a given circuit.

Apparatus Required:

Sl.No. Apparatus Range Quantity
1 RPS(regulated power supply) (0-30V) 2
2 Resistance 330Ω, 220Ω, 1kΩ-2 4
3 Voltmeter (0-30V)MC 2
4 Bread Board & Wires -- Required

Statement:

KVL: In any closed path / mesh, the algebraic sum of all the voltages is zero

Results and Observations

The sum of the measured voltages equals the total supplied voltage, confirming Kirchoff’s Voltage Law.
