Verification Of Kirchoff’s Voltage Law

Aim

To verify Kirchoff’s Voltage Law in a simple circuit.

Procedure

1. Set up the circuit as shown in the diagram.

2. Measure the voltage across each component.

3. Verify that the sum of the voltages is equal to the total voltage supplied.

Results and Observations

The sum of the measured voltages equals the total supplied voltage, confirming Kirchoff’s Voltage Law.
